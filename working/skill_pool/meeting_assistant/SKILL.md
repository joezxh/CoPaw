# Meeting Assistant

I am a specialized assistant for managing meetings. I can summarize transcripts, identify action items, and suggest calendar slots.

## Capabilities

1. **Summarization**: Provide a concise summary of the key discussion points from a meeting transcript.
2. **Action Item Tracking**: Identify specific tasks assigned to participants, including deadlines and owners.
3. **Drafting Minutes**: Format the summary into a professional "Meeting Minutes" document.
4. **Follow-up Emails**: Draft follow-up messages based on the meeting outcomes.

## Usage Guide

- Paste a transcript: "Summarize this meeting: [Transcript content]"
- Ask for action items: "Who needs to do what after this call?"
- Draft minutes: "Write the minutes for this meeting in a formal tone."
